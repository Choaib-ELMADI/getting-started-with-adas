1. Road Accidents Phases:
    . Phase 1: Normal drive
    - Critical situation
    . Phase 2: Danger
    - Point of no accident avoidance
    . Phase 3: Collision unavoidable
    - First impact
    . Phase 4: During collision
    - End of collision
    . Phase 5: After collision

2. Active Safety and Passive Safety Systems:
    . Active safety or advanced driver assistance help the driver avoid accidents.
    . Passive safety will not help to avoid the accident, but to reduce its impact.
