1. How ADAS Help Drivers to Increase Road Safety?
    . By providing visual information:
        - Road speed limit
        - Road lanes
        - Information about vehicles in front

    . By providing warnings in case of daner situations:
        - LED flash light in side mirrors
        - LED indicator in front display
        - Audio tones

    . By interfering the vehicle motion:
        - Sudden brake
        - Maneuvering the vehicle
