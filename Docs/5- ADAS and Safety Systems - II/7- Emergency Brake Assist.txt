1. Emergency Brake Assist:
    . An early-generation ADAS that enhances driver braking during emergency situations.
    . Helps reduce stopping distance when the driver brakes, but not hard enough or not fast enough.
    . Designed to prevent or mitigate front-end collisions, in sudden-stop or vulnerable-road-user scenarios.

2. Main Scenarios Where It Activates:
    . Sudden braking of the vehicle ahead.
    . Pedestrian suddenly entering the road.
    . Vehicle cutting in at close distance.
