1. Crosswind Stabilization Assist:
    . An ADAS feature that helps maintain lane position during strong side winds.
    . Automatically counteracts sudden lateral forces caused by gusts, preventing unintentional lane drift.
    . Particularly useful for high-sided vehicles (vans, SUVs, trucks) and on exposed roads.

2. Typical Situations where It Activates:
    . Exiting tunnels.
    . Driving on open bridges.
    . Passing or being passed by large trucks / buses.
    . Mountain passes or coastal roads with sudden wind changes.
    . High-speed driving in stormy conditions.
