1. Vehicle Exit Alert System:
    . Prevents dooring accidents.
    . Warns occupants before opening doors if vehicles, cyclists, or pedestrians are approaching from behind.
    . Protects passengers when exiting the parked / stopped vehicle.

2. Key Triggers & Operation:
    . Activates when vehicle is stopped (or ignition off) and a door is about to be opened.
    . Remains active for ~3 minutes after ignition is turned off.

3. Warning Methods:
    . Visual alerts:
        - Red LED contour lighting inside the door / door handle
        - High-intensity blinking red LEDs in critical situations
        - Side mirror or dashboard indicators
