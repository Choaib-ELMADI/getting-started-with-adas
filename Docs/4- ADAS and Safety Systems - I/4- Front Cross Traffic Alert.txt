1. Front Cross Traffic Alert (FCTA):
    . Detects vehicles, cyclists, or pedestrians approaching from the left / right when ego vehicle is moving slowly.
    . Prevents collisions at junctions where forward visibility is limited.

2. Activation Conditions:
    . Ego vehicle speed < ~10 km/h (6 mph).
    . Transmission in Drive (AT: D position, MT: any gear except N or R).

3. Driver Assistance Actions:
    . Audio and visual warnings (dashboard icon, HUD arrows, side mirror indicators).
    . Automatic emergency braking (AEB) if collision risk is imminent.
